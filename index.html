About Lodha Group

Established in 1980, Lodha Group has emerged as one of the premier real estate developers in Mumbai, renowned for its iconic landmarks and contributions to transforming the city's skyline. Led by an effective team of professionals equipped with technical expertise, extensive cross-functional experience, and excellent domain knowledge, the company has successfully delivered various developments in London, Mumbai, and Pune. With offices in New York, Dubai, Singapore, and Shanghai, Lodha Group is a multinational corporation that is committed to delivering excellence and quality in all its projects.
