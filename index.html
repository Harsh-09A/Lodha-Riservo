About L&T Realty

Established in 2011, l&t Realty is the real estate arm of the $21 billion Larsen and Toubro and a trendsetter amidst real estate developers in India. With an extensive portfolio spanning 70 mn. sqft. across Residential, Commercial and Retail developments, the company is currently present in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai. l&t Realty has delivered 47mn sqft. of Residential Development, 33 mn sqft of commercial & retail development, has 10,000+ happy families across 13+ Mixed Use & gated communities.
